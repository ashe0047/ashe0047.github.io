What is the effect of Vaccination Drive on the # of New Deaths?

Following the increase in vaccination rates over time, the rate of COVID death is increasing at a slower pace. After the peak in vaccination rate, the death rate can be seen decreasing

What about the Effectiveness of Testing Frequency on reducing # of New Cases?

As the pace of testing starts picking up in the year 2020, the rate of new infections was able to be kept stagnant for most of the year 2021. Moreover, as new variant emerge which is more infectious, this caused a spike in the number of new cases at the start of year 2022
